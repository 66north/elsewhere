Compatible spark plugs for the 6G75 3.8L V6 petrol engine (2008–2013 Pajero IV). You need 4 plugs per service. OEM spec: 14mm thread, 19mm reach, 1.0–1.1 mm gap, medium heat range. Replace every 60,000 km. OEM Mitsubishi part number is MD340648, equivalent to NGK 4867 and DENSO SK20PR-A8. Diesel 4M41 owners: see the Glow Plug reference instead.

## Tools needed

- 14mm spark plug socket (deep ceramic-padded type)
- 1/2" drive ratchet and extension
- Spark plug gap gauge (to verify 1.0–1.1 mm gap)
- Torque wrench

## Tips

- Always replace all 4 plugs as a set — mixing old and new causes uneven combustion.
- Pre-gap check: most plugs come set to 1.0 mm. Confirm before installation with a gap gauge.
- Hand-thread each plug first to avoid cross-threading the aluminium cylinder head.
- Worn spark plugs (60,000+ km) are the single most common cause of P0300 random misfire code.
- If replacing after misfire: also check coil packs and plug leads while you have access.
- Do not use carburettor cleaner or compressed air on plugs — inspecting them tells you engine condition (black = rich mixture, white = lean).
