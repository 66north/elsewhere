Navigate the highest passes of the Pyrenees in winter. The Pajero's weight distribution and low-range transfer case are advantages in snow. Plan for potential road closures and unpredictable weather.

## Key Passes
- Col du Glandon
- Col d'Aspin
- Pic du Midi
- Ordino-Arcalís (Andorra)

## Conditions
- Snow chains mandatory on many sections
- Avalanche risk: check METEO France before travel
- Accommodations: Valley towns fill quickly in winter weekends
