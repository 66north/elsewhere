The ultimate overland experience. Multi-week traverse of open Sahara, camping in the dunes, navigating by GPS and compass. The Pajero was built for this—ground clearance, durability, fuel capacity.

## Route Notes
- Start: Marrakech or Tamanrasset
- Stages: Ouarzazate → Tata → Tan Tan → Western Sahara → Mauritania
- Water: Must plan every stop carefully
- Fuel: Regional availability varies; some stages require jerry cans

## Vehicle Prep
- Suspension: Upgrade to intermediate springs or full coilover
- Fuel: Auxiliary tank essential
- Cooling: Upgraded radiator
- Navigation: Garmin or Sahara-specific GPS with offline maps
