The Mitsubishi Pajero (Montero / Shogun) was sold across three generations covered by this site (Gen 2, Gen 3, and Gen 4) with multiple engine options varying by market. Knowing which engine you have is the starting point for finding the right service information, parts numbers, and procedures. This guide summarises all engines by generation and helps you identify yours from the VIN, badge, or engine bay.

## Tips

- When ordering parts, always quote the full model code (e.g. V78W, V97W, V98W) rather than just the year — the same year may have different engines in different markets.
- The Gen 4 4M41 and Gen 3 4M41 share many parts but differ in the ECU mapping, injectors, and some accessory items.
- If you bought a used Pajero without service history, a vehicle history check by VIN or a dealer lookup will confirm the engine fitted.
