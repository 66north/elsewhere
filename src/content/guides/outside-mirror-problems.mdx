The Pajero IV outside mirrors have electric adjustment, optional heating, and optional electric folding. On vehicles with the ETACS (Electronic Time & Alarm Control System), the mirrors can be set to auto-fold when locking and unfold when unlocking. Mirror problems are usually electrical — a failed motor, a corroded connector in the door, or a blown fuse. The mirror adjustment switch is on the driver door trim; the fold switch (if fitted) is on the dash.

## Symptoms

- Mirror does not adjust in one or more directions — switch or motor fault
- Mirror does not fold or unfold electrically — fold motor or wiring
- Mirror heating not working in winter — check MIRROR HEAT fuse and heated mirror element
- Mirror position resets after folding — normal on ETACS vehicles (memorised to last switch position)
- Mirror glass cracked or fallen out — glass and motor/heater element replaceable separately

## Tools needed

- Trim removal tool (door card)
- Multimeter

## Tips

- Check the mirror fuse before anything else — the mirror circuit is typically labelled MIRROR or PWR MIRROR in the interior fuse box.
- Mirror heater fuse: separate to the adjustment motor circuit on most Pajero IV models. Labelled MIRROR HTR or DEFOG MIRROR.
- The door wiring harness passes through a flexible conduit in the door hinge area. This loom flexes every time the door opens and is the most common cause of intermittent mirror faults — wiggle the door through its full range while the mirror is selected to see if the fault appears or clears.
- To remove the mirror glass: press firmly at one edge and lever gently — it is clip-mounted onto the adjuster ball. Replacement glass snaps back in. No tools required if you are only replacing the glass.
- Mirror adjustment switch: if no mirrors move in any direction, the switch is suspect. Confirm 12V at the switch input. An OBD scanner with body functions can actuate mirrors directly if the switch is dead — useful for diagnosis.
- ETACS auto-fold setup: the fold-on-lock feature can be enabled or disabled via the customise menu in the instrument cluster (hold OK button on stalk during ignition on sequence — refer to owner handbook). Some personalisation settings require a dealer scan tool.
- A mirror that folds but does not unfold reliably is usually a fold motor with a worn park switch inside — the motor does not know when to stop and stalls.
