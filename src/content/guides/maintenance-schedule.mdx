Regular maintenance is the most cost-effective way to keep your Pajero IV running reliably and avoid expensive repairs. Mitsubishi specifies service intervals every 10,000 km or 12 months (whichever comes first) for most items. This guide covers the complete maintenance schedule from first service (1,000 km) through major services at 100,000+ km.

## Tips

- Keep detailed service records in your logbook — they prove maintenance history if you sell the vehicle.
- Don't skip major services to save money. A $300 service now prevents a $3,000 repair later.
- If you exceed the service interval significantly (20%+ over), still service the vehicle and inform the technician of the overdue work.
- Synthetic oils last longer (8,000-10,000 km intervals possible), but use factory-recommended viscosity.
- Have your mechanic explain any 'additional work required' — don't blindly approve it. Get a second opinion if the cost seems high.
- Dealer service is more expensive but uses genuine parts and warranty-backed labor. Independent workshops are cheaper but quality varies.
