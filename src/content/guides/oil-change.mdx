The most important routine maintenance on any Pajero IV. Fresh oil protects the engine, reduces wear, and maintains fuel economy. Mitsubishi recommends every 10,000 km or 12 months — whichever comes first. On the 4M41 diesel, use only ACEA C3-rated oil if your vehicle has a DPF (diesel particulate filter).

## Symptoms

- Routine maintenance interval reached (10,000 km / 12 months)
- Oil level low on dipstick
- Oil appears dark brown or black on dipstick
- Oil pressure warning light illuminated

## Tools needed

- Oil drain pan (8L+ capacity)
- 17mm socket or ring spanner (drain plug)
- Oil filter removal strap wrench
- Funnel
- Torque wrench

## Tips

- Run the engine for 2–3 minutes before draining. Warm oil flows faster and carries more debris.
- 4M41 capacity: 6.5L without filter, 7.0L with new filter. Always check dipstick and top up accordingly.
- Pre-fill the new oil filter with fresh oil before fitting — reduces dry-start duration.
- Let the car sit on level ground for 5 minutes after filling before reading the dipstick.
- Dispose of used oil responsibly — most auto parts stores accept it.
